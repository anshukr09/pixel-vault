Our Story

About PIXELVAULT

Born from a love of gaming and a frustration with overpriced titles, PIXELVAULT exists to make great games accessible to every gamer — at a price that doesn't hurt.

Who We Are

Built by Gamers,
for Gamers

PIXELVAULT started in 2019 as a small operation out of a dorm room — a few shelves, a laptop, and a mission: make gaming more affordable and sustainable. What began as a personal project turned into one of the most trusted preowned gaming platforms in the country.

We believe that a game's value doesn't expire the moment someone finishes it. Every title in our library has brought joy to at least one gamer already — and through PIXELVAULT, it gets to do so again. We're proud to offer a circular economy for games, where stories keep being told and cartridges keep spinning.

Our team of 20+ passionate gamers hand-inspects every single game before it goes on sale. We don't just sell discs — we sell experiences, memories, and adventures. That's why every item comes with our Quality Guarantee: if it's not perfect, we make it right.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Values

Three core principles shape how we serve gamers every single day.

♻️
Sustainability

Gaming has an environmental footprint. By giving preowned games a second life, we reduce electronic waste and help build a more sustainable gaming industry — one game at a time.

🤝
Fairness

We believe in fair prices for buyers and sellers alike. Our trade-in valuations are honest, our retail prices are transparent, and we never hide fees or surprises at checkout.

Gaming is better together. We're more than a marketplace — we're a gathering place for people who love games, share recommendations, debate best titles, and celebrate gaming culture.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a tiny dorm room project to a full-scale gaming marketplace — the PIXELVAULT timeline.

2019 — The Beginning
PIXELVAULT is Founded

Started as a personal buy-sell operation among college friends. The demand was overwhelming from day one, and it was clear this could be something bigger.

2020 — Going Digital
Website & Online Store Launch

Built the first version of the PIXELVAULT website, launched online orders, and implemented our first quality inspection system. Within six months we had 500+ transactions and zero complaints.

2021 — Trade-In System
Introducing the Trade Program

Launched our official trade-in program — allowing gamers to bring or ship in their games and receive fair store credit. This became our most popular feature and is now the backbone of our business.

2022 — 10,000 Games
Hitting Major Milestones

Hit 10,000 active listings, onboarded our 20,000th customer, and expanded to cover all major platforms. Hired a full team of dedicated game inspectors and customer support staff.

2023 — Community Hub
The PIXELVAULT Community

Launched community features — game reviews, user wishlists, trading forums, and monthly gaming events. The platform evolved from a marketplace into a destination for gaming culture.

2025 — Present
Growing Stronger Every Day

Today, PIXELVAULT is home to 48,000+ registered gamers, 12,000+ game titles, and processes 5,000+ trades every month. Still growing, still improving, still obsessed with great gaming.
