Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying, trading, and selling at PIXELVAULT.

Contact us with a list of your games (title + platform + condition). We'll send a fair credit quote within 24 hours. If you accept, ship them in or drop off at our location. Once games pass our 5-step inspection, credit is added to your account immediately.
We accept games in Good or better condition — meaning the disc plays without issues and the case is present. Severely scratched discs or missing cases are accepted at a reduced rate. We'll always give you a quote before you commit to anything.
Orders placed before midnight are dispatched same day. Standard delivery takes 2–4 business days nationwide. You'll receive a tracking link via email as soon as your order ships. Express delivery is available at checkout for an extra fee.
Our 30-day warranty covers any functional defect — a disc that won't read, a cartridge that won't boot, or any hardware issue on physical media. If a game doesn't work, contact us and we'll ship a replacement or issue a full refund. No questions asked.
