The Process

How PIXELVAULT Works

Three ways to interact — all designed to make gaming affordable and fun.

🛒
Buy Preowned

Browse our vault of 12,000+ tested games. Filter by platform, genre, or price. Every game is graded — Mint, Good, or Fair — so you always know what you're getting before you buy.

🔄
Trade Your Games

Bring in games you've finished and trade for store credit. Our fair evaluators assess every title and give an honest market-rate value. Upgrade your collection for almost nothing.

💰
Sell to Us

Don't want store credit? Sell your games directly and get paid. Submit a list, get a quote in 24 hours, ship them in, and receive payment once they pass inspection.

⭐
Wishlist & Alerts

Can't find a specific game? Add it to your wishlist and we'll alert you the moment it arrives. Never miss a title you've been hunting for again.

🏷️
Bundle Deals

Buy 3 games and get the cheapest one free. Mix and match any titles across any platform. Our bundle discounts stack automatically at checkout — no codes needed.

🔒
30-Day Warranty

Every game comes with a 30-day functional warranty. If a disc doesn't work, we replace it or give a full refund. No questions. No arguments.
